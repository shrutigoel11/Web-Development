IRCTC Train Ticket Booking Online

Here’s how to book IRCTC tickets online: 1.Open the ixigo website or download mobile app. Click on book train tickets section.
2.Select the source and destination Indian Railway stations from the list.
3.Pick a date of departure and travel class-AC First Class, Sleeper Class, Second AC, Third AC, AC Chair car.
4.Choose your Indian Railway train and travel class based on arrivel time and travel time (in hours).
5.Fill in the passenger details including berth perference, names of children, senior citizens.
6.Log in using your IRCTC user ID and password. Create a new IRCTC username if you don't have one.
7.Complete the payment and reservation process using your IRCTC login details. You will be redirected to IRCTC website.
8.You will then receive a confirmation on your registered email id and SMS on mobile number.
